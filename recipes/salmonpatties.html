SALMON PATTIES

Salmon patties are a great dinner idea for the whole family to enjoy

This recipe was a staple of my childhood. My dad would often have my siblings and I helping him make dinner, and salmon patties was definitely a favorite in our family. Even my picky eater brother enjoyed them!

The best part is they are an easy weeknight dinner. Well, actually...the best part is that they taste fantastic! The crispy outside is just perfection and I like to dip mine in honey or maple syrup for a sweet finish.

    Ingredients

  • 1 (14.75 ounce) can of Salmon
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup seasoned dry bread crumbs
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

    Steps

  1. Drain and reserve liquid from salmon. Mix egg, onion, bread crumbs, and salmon together
  2. Make into patties. If mixturre is too dry to form patties, add some of the reserved liquid
  3. In a frying pan, heat oil. Place patties in a pan. Brown on each side, turning gently. Drain on paper towels and serve.
